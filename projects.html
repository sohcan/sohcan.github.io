Featured Project Queue

Optimization

QUBO Budget Optimizer

A synthetic project-selection model using Quadratic Unconstrained Binary Optimization (QUBO) to choose the best mix of facility projects under a fixed budget.

Planned features: project table, budget slider, selected-project summary, feasible/infeasible status, and comparison against a greedy baseline.

Dashboard

Utility / Facilities Dashboard

A synthetic utility analytics dashboard for monthly kilowatt-hours, total cost, blended rate, rolling averages, site comparison, and anomaly flags.

Planned features: monthly trend cards, site-level charts, rate movement, and plain-English interpretation of operational data.

Quantum Demo

Elitzur–Vaidman Bomb Tester

An interactive explanation of quantum interference, measurement, and interaction-free detection using a Mach–Zehnder-style toy model.

Planned features: scenario selector, detector outcomes, probability summary, and Qiskit circuit notebook.
